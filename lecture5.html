Lecture 5: Media and Multi-Page Websites

1. Media Tag

The media tag allows you to integrate audio and video files into your website. It is important for adding multimedia content to make websites more interactive.


2. Integrating Audio Files

Use the <audio> tag to add audio to your webpage.

Syntax:

 <audio src="audio-file.mp3"></audio>

Example:

 <audio src="music.mp3"></audio>
